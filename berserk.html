About Berserk

"Berserk" is a dark fantasy anime based on the manga series by Kentaro Miura. It follows the story of Guts, a lone mercenary, as he battles against demonic forces in a medieval-inspired world.

Characters

Some of the main characters in Berserk include Guts, Griffith, Casca, and many others.

Episodes

The anime adaptation of Berserk consists of multiple seasons and covers various story arcs from the manga.
